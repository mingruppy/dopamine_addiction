DOPAMINE_ADDICTION

Dopamine is a neurotransmitter secreted into the nerve circuits responsible for motivation and pleasure. Dopamine poisoning is a phenomenon in which dopamine, which is involved in reward and pleasure, becomes increasingly insensitive, and finds more stimulating and stronger pleasure.

Side effects

What are the changes we have caused by dopamine addiction?

tolerance

Our bodies become resistant to stimuli if we are exposed to simple but large pleasures many times. Even with the same stimuli as before, we cannot feel pleasure. These changes change us to pursue greater stimuli.

depression

Our standard of living has improved a lot in modern times. However, the index of depression is going up. While avoiding pain and seeking only pleasure, hedonism is spreading with depression in our society.

addiction

As the pursuit of any substance or action intensifies, symptoms of addiction arise. Addiction not only means simply falling into the action, but also affects the daily lives of me and those around me.

detox

How to Get Away With Dopamine Addiction

mind-binding

It's self-control. It's distancing itself from simple pleasures such as smartphones, sweet foods, and drugs.

the pursuit of pain

Experiencing pain. Here, pain is the process of not simply obtaining pleasure. For example, you go through a process that entails pain, such as exercise, reading, studying, and practicing musical instruments. After going through such a process, the body heads toward happiness by homeostasis

focus on yourself

Focus on yourself and gain insight from your actions. It is difficult to obtain these insights if you have an addicted substance.
